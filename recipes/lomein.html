Lo mein

Enjoy your favorite chinese dish of Lo mein with a Desi twist!

Quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • 200 grams chicken
  • 1/4 tsp pepper powder
  • 1/8 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 to 1 tsp five spice
  • 1 tsp oil
  • salt
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 tbsps spring onion
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup carrots
  • 1/4 cup cabbage
  • 1 Â½ tbsp chilli sauce

Steps

    Heat a pan with 1 tbsp oil
    Add garlic and fry for 30 seconds
    Add chicken and saute on medium heat until it is cooked thoroughly. If pan gets dry add 1 to 2 tbsps of hot water. Cold water will harden chicken
    Cut a piece of chicken and make sure it is cooked. Set aside chicken
    Add another tbsp of oil to the pan. Regulate the flame to the highest temperature
    Add spring onions and saute them for a minute
    Add in cut up veggies, bell pepperm carrots and cabbage
    Add spring onion greens, chilli sauces, noodles,soy sauce. If needed add a dash of salt
    Briefly fry for a minute or two, if noodles look dry add oil
    Serve and enjoy chicken noodles hot :)!
